Air Squared

Develops oil-free scroll compressors and vacuum pumps

Product Line Capacity Increase

  • Role: Lead Engineer
  • Summary: Increase the production capacity of vacuum pump units from 12 to 50 units per day.
  • Scope: Identify the largest bottlenecks, eliminate the need for "shimming", automate the testing certification, build new test and burn-in stands.
  • Process: The biggest bottleneck for increasing production was building a larger test stand because of the overnight testing requirement and more details are in the next section. The next most time-consuming process was "shimming" and improving this process involved meeting with design engineers and reviewing data to find a new standard measurement. Up until I began working there, all the testing sheets were pdf or paper, which made collecting data difficult. As a result I decided to make an automated Excel sheet that made testing easier and also sent the data to a larger Excel sheet so it could be easily searched for later. Finally, to save time and reduce environmental impact, I designed an improved packaging for the product.
  • Skills: Excel VBA, Packaging Engineering, Lean Methodology
  • Results: The product line was producing more than 50 units a day ahead of schedule.

Overnight Burn-in Stand

  • Role: Lead Engineer
  • Summary: To increase the production capacity of the product line, the burn-in stand needed to be able to accommodate 50 units.
  • Scope: Design and build a burn-in stand with a power supply and motor controllers
  • Process: I began with identifying the power supplies I needed and motor controllers. From there I crimped and wired all the motor controllers. One of the final challenges was designing custom supports to dampen the vibration of each vacuum pump.
  • Skills: Electrical engineering, wiring, crimping
  • Results: The final stand was operating successfully.
